About This Project

I created this project to raise awareness about mental health disparities among Black women. As a Black woman, I have witnessed and experienced some of the challenges firsthand, and I hope to educate others and inspire change.

Key Findings

  • Superwoman Schema: Pressures Black women to appear strong, discouraging them from seeking help.
  • Stigma: Mental health is often dismissed in Black communities, making it hard to seek support.
  • Lack of Representation: Few diverse mental health providers leads to mistrust and poor care.
  • Structural Barriers: Financial instability, transportation issues, and systemic racism worsen access to care.
